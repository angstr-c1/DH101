Sustainability & Ethics

I use AI sustainably by treating it as a tool to support my learning and creativity rather than replace my own thinking. I rely on AI to help organize ideas, improve efficiency, and explore new perspectives, while still verifying information, thinking critically, and completing the final work myself. By using AI responsibly and intentionally, I aim to balance innovation with originality, ethics, and meaningful human input.
